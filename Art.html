Meditation With My Hands

There's a specific pleasure and satisfaction that comes from the act of creation. It's not so much the end result, but rather the journey - decision making, problem solving and unexpected difficulties. It's a moment of pure joy when everything clicks into place for me and I have a completed work I can be proud of.

Painting

As with most people, I started drawing at an early age. In my younger school years I worked with watercolors, pastels and other media before concentrating on acrylics. I have painted wall murals, custom set piece graphics, and canvas art.

Body Art

I learned henna body art - a kind of temporary tattoo - in 1999 when it was a young industry. I studied many global styles, techniques and recipes from the tight-knit online community and meetings with local artists. Though private appointments with clients are always enjoyable, I find the social environments of parties and restaurant clients far more satisfying.

Set Construction

When I worked for a home decor importer, I saw that a beautiful set would enhance any product photo. Because of budget limitations, I quickly adapted to adding construction, molding and installation to my graphic design skills. This allowed the team to plan and build gorgeous sets during downtime to be ready for the upcoming catalog production.
