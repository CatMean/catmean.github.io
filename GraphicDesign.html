Lovely Bones

The aesthetics - typography, color and structure - are the glittering visible parts of graphic design, but without careful thought and understanding of the speaker, message and audience, such visuals are quickly forgotten and won't deliver a satisfactory return on investment. In my experience as well, proficiency in the Creative Cloud can make any designer execute a client's vision with lightning speed.

Speed and Preparation is Key

Much of my experience developed in the 12 years producing holiday catalogs for a home decor importer. Since the team and I started photography and layout before the final product decisions were finalized, I needed to become proficient in InDesign to quickly allow for text and image changes. Establishing aesthetic guidelines and templates helped us make fast decisions and effective redesign of pages regardless of the amount of content.

Print is Not Dead

In my daily life, every business I experience has a need for printed material. In my career, I was lucky to come from a print production background with an understanding of color requirements, best practices and pre-press techniques. Having this background allowed me to convert any working design into printed products with little or no loss in quality.
